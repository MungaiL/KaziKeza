# Employee Task Tracking System

**Type:** Project Management Tooling
**Tool:** Microsoft Excel/Google Sheets
**Timeline:** 2026

---

## What is this?

A project management tool built entirely on Google Sheets, designed for team
leads who need to assign, monitor, and report on team tasks without relying on
paid software. It includes automated dashboards, a structured task log, and
weekly reporting templates.

---

## The problem it solved

Managing a cross functional team of 20+ people across multiple departments meant tracking dozens of tasks, owners, deadlines, and statuses
simultaneously. Off-the-shelf tools used a subscription pricing models and were not custom to the organization's needs. I built a custom system that worked for the organisation's need and
could be handed to any team member to update.

---

## What I built

- **Task Log** — structured sheet for logging tasks with owner, deadline,
  priority level, and current status
- **Status Tracking** — lifecycle states: Not Started / In Progress /
  Complete / Blocked
- **Automated Dashboard** — summary view using Excel formulas and conditional
  formatting, updating live as tasks are edited
- **Weekly Report Template** — pre-formatted snapshot for stakeholder reporting
- **Config Tab** — centralised team member list that populates dropdowns
  across the workbook
- **Project Overview** — a seamless way for every employee to quickly view active projects and receive project instructions
